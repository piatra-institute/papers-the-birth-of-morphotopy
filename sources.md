- Gibbs, J. W. (1902). *Elementary Principles in Statistical Mechanics*. Charles Scribner's Sons. — phase space as the geometry of possible states; §5.

- Landauer, T. K., & Dumais, S. T. (1997). A solution to Plato's problem: The latent semantic analysis theory of acquisition, induction, and representation of knowledge. *Psychological Review*, 104(2), 211–240. — meaning as co-occurrence geometry; §6.
- Levin, M. (2022). - Pauling, L. (1935). The structure and entropy of ice... *Journal of the American Chemical Society*, 57(12), 2680–2684. — residual ground-state entropy; §7 frustration.
- Poincaré, H. (1895). Analysis Situs. *Journal de l'École Polytechnique*, (2)1, 1–123. — topology beneath measurement; §4.
- Raup, D. M. (1966). Geometric analysis of shell coiling: General problems. *Journal of Paleontology*, 40(5), 1178–1190. — the founding morphospace; §6. Locator verified.
- Riemann, B. (1868). Über die Hypothesen, welche der Geometrie zu Grunde liegen. *Abhandlungen der Königlichen Gesellschaft der Wissenschaften zu Göttingen*, 13, 133–150. (Habilitation lecture delivered 1854.) — intrinsic space, manifold, curvature; §3. Deliver/print dates both noted.
